ABOUT US

Welcome to Ankle Breaker, your go-to destination for original footwear, stylish socks, and trendy hats. Nestled in the heart of the Public Market in Dili, Gasan, Marinduque, our shop is dedicated to providing customers with high-quality products that redefine comfort, style, and individuality. At Ankle Breaker, we believe that shoes, socks, and hats are more than just accessories; they are statements of personal expression. Our collection boasts an array of unique designs, carefully curated to cater to diverse tastes and preferences. With a passion for offering originality and comfort, we source our products from reputable brands and independent designers who prioritize craftsmanship and innovation. Each item in our store is selected with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring that our customers receive exceptional quality and style. Whether you're looking for the perfect pair of sneakers, cozy and vibrant socks, or a statement hat to complete your look, Ankle Breaker is here to elevate your style game. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff are dedicated to assisting you in finding the ideal pieces that reflect your individuality. Join us at Ankle Breaker and experience the fusion of fashion, comfort, and authenticity. Visit our store at the Public Market in Dili, Gasan, Marinduque, and step into a world where your style knows no bounds.
